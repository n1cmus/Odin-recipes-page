Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This classic italian dish is much loved around the world, easy to cook, 10 mins prep time and 1 hour cook time. Only thing left to do is decide which italian wine to choose!

Ingredients List

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion finely chopped
  • 2 celery sticks finely chopped
  • 2 carrots finely chopped
  • 50g pancetta cubes
  • 400g lean beef mince
  • 150g chicken livers chopped, fat and sinew removed
  • 1 large bay leaves
  • 4 tbsp tomato puree
